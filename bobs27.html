Bob's 27:

Game Play: Bob's 27 was designed as a practice game for players who want to strengthen hitting doubles, which is a necessity for completeing a game of X01. Each player begins by shooting 3 darts into the double 1, next round the double 2 and so on until bulls-eye.

Scoring: Each player begins the game with 27 points. Players are awarded points for each successful double hit on the number they are shooting for (ie. A player shooting at the 1, hits all 3 doubles then they get 6 points). If no doubles are hit on a turn then the player loses the amount of points for one double (ie. If a player is shooting at the 1 and miss all 3 darts then they lose 2 points.) If a player reaches 0 or less points then the game is over for that player, and players with remaining points continue on. the maximum amount of points a player can recieve is 1437.

Just as an FYI, your score should always be an odd number.
